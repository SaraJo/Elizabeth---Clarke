1.
where is my order?

click ‘my order’ in the top, right corner; this will take you to PayPal’s website. you can also access your account by visiting www.paypal.com and logging into your account. once you are signed-in to PayPal, you may review your order details including ship date, payment amount, current subscription status, and refund status, if applicable

2.
how do i cancel my subscription?

click ‘my order’ in the top, right corner; this will take you to PayPal’s website. you can also access your account by visiting www.paypal.com and logging into your account. once you are signed-in to PayPal, you may view your previous orders, make changes, or cancel your subscription. if you are having difficulties with your account, please contact PayPal at +1 888 221 1161

3.
how do i return an item?

simply email us at info@elizabethandclarke.com and request a refund, we will respond with a return shipping address and pre-paid shipping label. when your items arrive at elizabeth & clarke, we will issue a full refund through PayPal for the cost of the shirt(s)

4.
is my credit card information safe?

if there is a problem with your order, you are protected when you pay with PayPal. PayPal offers buyers:

  • $0 liability for Eligible Unauthorized Purchases when you meet PayPal's requirements
  • refunds for incorrect orders or items that never arrive
  • a convenient process for resolving problems
Check out the PayPal Purchase Protection Overview for more details.

6.
to which countries do you ship?

we ship everywhere UPS and the US Postal Service ships: to over 200 countries worldwide. all domestic shipping within the United States (both outbound and return) is free. there is a $10 surcharge for all international shipping

7.
how much taxes and duties do i have to pay?

in New York State, we pay the sales tax for you, it is included in the price of your box. for all other states and international shipments, please check with your accountant or local tax authorities to understand what you may owe in duties and taxes. ultimately, all duties and taxes are the responsibility of the end consumer and we assume no liability in this area

9.
when will payment be deducted from my PayPal account?

payment is deducted from your account annually. the first payment is deducted the same day you place your first order. subsequent payments are deducted annually, according to your subscription, on the first of the month. for example: if you choose a two season annual subscription for fall and spring, your total annual payment of $149 will be deducted from your PayPal account on August 1st.
